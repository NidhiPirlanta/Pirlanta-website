What is the Pirlanta Cybersecurity Assessment?

The Pirlanta Cybersecurity Assessment helps you understand where you stand in your digital security journey. It evaluates how cyber-ready your business is and identifies areas that need strengthening to protect against emerging threats.

This assessment analyzes your organization across three critical dimensions: Secure Customer Engagement, Secure Digital Workplace, and Secure Digital Operations. Based on your responses, we provide personalized insights and recommendations to strengthen your security posture.
