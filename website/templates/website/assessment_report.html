What is the Digital Assessment?

The Pirlanta Digital Assessment helps you understand where you stand in your digital journey. It's designed to help you understand how future-ready your business is and suggests areas of your business that need to be digitalized.

This comprehensive evaluation analyzes your business across three critical dimensions: Digital Customers, Digital Workplace, and Digital Operations. Based on your responses, we provide personalized insights and recommendations to accelerate your digital transformation.
